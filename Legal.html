Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025
Keziah Accommodation is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), Act 4 of 2013 (South Africa).

1. Who We Are

Keziah Accommodation ("we", "us", "our") operates self-catering accommodation at 97 Wehmeyer Street, Willowmore, Karoo, South Africa. We are the responsible party for the personal information we collect from you.

2. What Information We Collect

When you make a booking or contact us, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Booking dates and room preferences
  • Payment reference information
  • Any additional information you voluntarily provide

We also collect limited technical data when you visit our website, such as your browser type and pages visited, through standard web technology.

3. Why We Collect Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Process and confirm your accommodation booking
  • Communicate with you about your stay
  • Respond to enquiries and support requests
  • Send booking confirmations and relevant updates
  • Comply with legal obligations

We will not use your information for unsolicited marketing without your consent.

4. How We Store and Protect Your Information

Your personal information is stored securely and is only accessible to authorised personnel. We take reasonable technical and organisational steps to prevent unauthorised access, loss, or misuse of your data.

We retain your booking information for a reasonable period as required for business records and legal compliance, after which it is securely deleted.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or trade your personal information. We may share it only where required by law, or with service providers who assist in operating our business (such as payment processors), under strict confidentiality obligations.

6. Your Rights Under POPIA

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Object to the processing of your information
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa

7. Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience. Some third-party services we use (such as Google Fonts and Font Awesome) may also set cookies. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: May 2026
We understand that plans can change. Please read our cancellation policy carefully before making a booking, as it forms part of your agreement with us.

1. Cancellation by the Guest

  • More than 14 days before arrival: Full refund of any deposit paid, minus a 10% administration fee.
  • 7 to 14 days before arrival: 50% of the total booking value will be forfeited.
  • Less than 7 days before arrival: No refund will be issued.
  • No-show: The full booking amount will be forfeited.

2. How to Cancel

All cancellations must be submitted in writing via WhatsApp or email. Cancellations are only confirmed once acknowledged by us in writing. The cancellation date will be based on the date we receive written notification.

3. Refund Processing

Where a refund is due, it will be processed within 7 to 10 business days to the original payment method used.

4. Cancellation by Keziah

In the unlikely event that we need to cancel your booking due to circumstances beyond our control (force majeure, unforeseen property issues, etc.), you will receive a full refund of any payments made. We will make every effort to notify you as early as possible and to assist in finding alternative accommodation.

5. Changes to Bookings

Requests to change booking dates are subject to availability and must be made in writing. We will do our best to accommodate changes at no extra charge, but this cannot be guaranteed.

6. Consumer Protection Act (CPA)

Under the Consumer Protection Act 68 of 2008, consumers may have additional rights regarding cancellations. In the event of a conflict between this policy and the CPA, the CPA will take precedence.
